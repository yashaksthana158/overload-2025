Format & Winning Criteria

Format

  • Total Rounds: 4
  • Round 1 - Scrabble: Words must be aligned in a single row or column.
  • Round 2 - Crossword: Players fill in a grid with words matching given clues, connecting them correctly.
  • Round 3 - Word Find: Participants must locate hidden words within a time limit.
  • Final Round - Jigsaw: An image will be displayed for 10 seconds before participants solve the puzzle.

Scoring System

  • Scrabble: Winner earns 2 points.
  • Crossword: Teams of 4, with the winning team earning 4 points (1 per player).
  • Word Find: Teams of 2, with winners receiving 4 points each.
  • Jigsaw: Finalists compete individually for first place.

Winning Criteria

  • The finalist with the highest performance in the Jigsaw round wins.
  • In case of a tie, an additional puzzle challenge will be given.
