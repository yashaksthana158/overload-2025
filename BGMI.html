Rules & Guidelines

  • Eligibility & Registration:
    • All participants must register before the event. On-spot registration will be subject to availability.
    • Players must provide their BGMI ID and in-game name during registration.
    • Only squads (4 players) are allowed. No solo, duo, or trio teams are permitted.
    • Participants must be at least Level 30 and Platinum V rank or above in BGMI.
  • In-Game Rules:
    • Teaming up with other squads is strictly prohibited. Violators will be disqualified for that match.
    • Third-party software (hacks, scripts, macros, etc.) is banned. If detected, the entire squad will be immediately disqualified.
    • Players are allowed to use chargers, headgear, and cooling fans. No additional gaming accessories (triggers, external controllers, etc.) are allowed.
    • Emulator players are not allowed. Only mobile devices are permitted.
    • Use of in-game glitches or exploits is forbidden. Intentional abuse may result in disqualification.
  • Match Conduct:
    • Players must join the custom lobby within 5 minutes of receiving the room ID and password.
    • Any network or device issues faced by a player will not result in a rematch. Players are responsible for their own connectivity.
    • Voice chat must be kept respectful. Any form of harassment, toxicity, or foul language will lead to penalties or disqualification.
    • In case of technical server issues, match restarts will be at the discretion of the event coordinators.

Format & Winning Criteria

    Format

    • Total Matches: 2
    • Match Duration: 25-30 minutes per match
    • Only full squads (4 players) allowed. Duos or trios are not permitted.
    • Game Mode: Standard Battle Royale
    • Map Selection: Randomized before each match

    Format & Scoring

    Device & Connectivity:
    • Players must use mobile devices only.
    • Emulators and tablets are strictly prohibited.
    • Participants must use their own mobile data.
    • Players should bring their own power banks or charging devices.
    • College Wi-Fi will not be provided.

    Scoring System

    • Placement Points:
      • Position Points
      1st Place10 Points
      2nd Place6 Points
      3rd Place5 Points
      4th Place4 Points
      5th Place3 Points
      6th Place2 Points
      7th - 8th Place1 Point
      9th - 25th Place0 Points
    • Kill Points: Each kill earns 1 point.

    Winning Criteria

    • The team with the highest cumulative points after 2 matches wins.
    • Points are calculated based on placement and kills.
    • In case of a tie, the team with the highest number of total kills will be declared the winner.
    • If the tie persists, the team with the highest placement in the last match will win.
