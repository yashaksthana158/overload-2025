Format & Winning Criteria

Tournament Format

  • Single Elimination Tournament.
  • Once a player loses a match, they are eliminated.
  • The winner of each match advances to the next round until the final match determines the champion.

Match Structure

  • Each match consists of multiple rounds.
  • Standard matches: Best of 3 rounds (First to win 2 rounds wins the match).
  • Final match: Best of 5 rounds (First to win 3 rounds wins the match).
  • Each round has a 99-second timer.

How to Win a Round

  • Completely deplete the opponentâ€™s health bar before time runs out.
  • If the timer reaches zero, the player with the higher remaining health wins the round.
  • If a Draw occurs (both players have equal health when time expires), an additional single round is played to decide the winner.

How to Win a Match

  • Win 2 out of 3 rounds in standard matches.
  • Win 3 out of 5 rounds in the Final match.

How to Win the Tournament

  • The tournament follows a knockout bracket format.
  • Each match winner advances to the next stage.
  • The last player remaining in the bracket is crowned the Tekken Showdown Champion.
