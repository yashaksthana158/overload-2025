Format & Winning Criteria

Format

  • Total Rounds: 3 (Easy, Medium, Hard)
  • Question Distribution:
    • Easy Round: 10 questions, 30 seconds per question
    • Medium Round: 10 questions, 45 seconds per question
    • Hard Round: 10 questions, 60 seconds per question
  • Team Structure:
    • Participants compete in pairs (teams of 2 students).
    • Each team has one buzzer to answer questions.
  • Buzzer System:
    • The first team to press the buzzer within the time limit gets to answer.
    • If the answer is correct, they earn points; if wrong, they lose points.

Scoring System

  • Easy Round: +1 point per correct answer
  • Medium Round: +3 points per correct answer
  • Hard Round: +5 points per correct answer
  • Wrong Answer Penalty: -1 point deduction

Winning Criteria

  • The team with the highest total points after all 3 rounds wins.
  • If there is a tie, a sudden-death question round will decide the winner.
